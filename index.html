How we do that?

Monthly Prayer Sessions

We organize Bible and Prayer Sessions every month in order to allow you meet more people and discuss issues concerning our spiritual lives as well as pray during this period

Group Discussions

During our fasting period, we organize Group Discussions to debate on issues troubling the world today and how we can practically use the Word to battle these adversities

Online Meetings

As our main form of communication due to our widespread reach, we use the internet to its fullest in order to organize our prayer and Bible meetings

Help Forum

In our group chat, we discuss topics to help you grow your faith in Christ such as love, peace, honesty and many more to let you understand the Word in a better light

    Learn how to Pray?

    1

    Connect and Prepare

    Find a quiet place, clear your mind, and focus on God. You can start by expressing gratitude, acknowledging His presence, or taking a deep breath to center yourself

    2

    Speak from the heart

    Talk to God honestly about your thoughts, feelings, and needs. This can include thanking Him, asking for guidance, seeking forgiveness, or praying for others

    3

    Listen and Reflect

    After speaking, take a moment to be silent and open your heart to any insights, peace, or direction. You can also read scripture or meditate on a meaningful verse

    Suggested Prayers

    For Love;

    Dear God, Fill my heart with love and kindness. Help me to give and receive love freely, with a heart full of gratitude and joy. Guide me toward a love that is pure and true.Amen.

    For Peace;

    Heavenly Father, Bring peace to our hearts, our homes, and our world. Let love and understanding replace fear and division. May Your light guide us toward unity and harmony. Amen.

    For Anxiety;

    Heavenly Father, When fear overwhelms me, remind me that You are near. Give me courage to face each moment with faith. Let Your peace replace my worries. Amen.
