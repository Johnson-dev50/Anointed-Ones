Experience Christianity in a way you have never done before, the right atmosphere and systems put in place to help you grow spiritually!

Some tips to help you get used to Prayer

1

Find a quiet Space

Choose a peaceful place where you can focus without distractions.

2

Speak from the Heart

Talk to God in your own words or use a traditional prayer. Express gratitude, ask for help, or simply share your thoughts.

3

Listen and Reflect

Prayer isn't just about talkingâ€”it's also about being still, listening, and feeling connected.

4

Make it a Habit

The more you pray, the more natural it becomes. Try to set a regular time each day to build consistency

Bible Plan

Below are three different Bible reading plans depending on how intense you want to read and understand the Word of the Lord

Three day Bible Plan

Best for beginners and those with busy schedules

  • Read 4-5 Chapters for three days weekly
  • Day One and Two(Old Testaments & Psalms & Day Three New Testaments)
  • Complete the entire Bible within a yea

    • FIve day Bible Plan

    Recommended for people already in the faith trying to keep it steady

  • Read 4-5 Chapters for five days weekly
  • Day One - Three(Old Testaments & Psalms) Day Four & Five (New Testaments)
  • Complete the entire Bible within a yea

    • Seven day Bible Plan

    For those going all in and plan on not stopping {Anointed Recommended}

  • Read 4-5 Chapters everyday
  • Day One - Four(Old Testaments & Psalms) Day Four - Day Seven (New Testaments)
  • Complete the entire Bible within a yea
